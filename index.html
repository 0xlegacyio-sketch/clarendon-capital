FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is your platform?

Your financial assets are held with a licensed insurance custodian for safekeeping, preventing misappropriation, misuse, theft, and loss in accordance with applicable law. We maintain detailed records of all investments.

Do you offer individually tailored plans?

Yes. We work closely with each client to craft an investment portfolio custom-tailored to their goals, current financial situation, and risk preference. No two plans are the same.

What industries do you specialize in?

We are global real estate and asset management service providers, offering investment services across REITs, residential and commercial real estate, Forex, CFDs on indices, metals, energies, bonds, stocks, and cryptocurrencies.

What is the minimum investment amount?

Minimum investment thresholds vary by plan. We offer accessible entry points for new investors as well as institutional-grade services for larger portfolios. Contact us for a personalized assessment.

How do I get started?

Simply open a free account online or request a callback from one of our advisers. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your goals and present the most suitable investment plan.
