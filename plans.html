FAQ

Plan Questions

Can't find your answer? Contact us and we'll get back to you within one business day.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade your plan at any time by depositing additional funds to reach the next tier's minimum. Your adviser will contact you to confirm the upgrade and update your portfolio strategy.

How do I withdraw my funds?

Withdrawals are processed via cryptocurrency to your verified wallet address. Submit a withdrawal request through your dashboard. Processing times vary by plan: Elite (same-day), Premium (48h), Growth/Starter (5–7 business days).

Which cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We currently accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT (TRC-20 and ERC-20), and USDC (ERC-20). All amounts are converted to USD equivalent at the time of deposit.

How long does deposit confirmation take?

Deposits are typically confirmed within 1–24 hours depending on network congestion and the number of blockchain confirmations required. Our team manually verifies each transaction for security.
