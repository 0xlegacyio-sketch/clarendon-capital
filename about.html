Our Story

Built on Expertise, Driven by Results

Founded over two decades ago, Clarendon Capital has grown from a boutique real estate advisory into a full-spectrum global investment platform — trusted by hundreds of clients across four continents.

We operate under the Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) trademark, providing access to curated real estate assets alongside diversified financial products. Our Investment Committee brings decades of collective experience to every decision.

  • Global REIT-certified investment platform
  • $370M+ in assets under management
  • 408+ successfully completed projects
  • 12 international awards for excellence
  • External audits for full regulatory compliance
